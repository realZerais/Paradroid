How To Play

Back

Gameplay: You are being hunted by a cyborg police and you have to destroy it.

Mechanics: You and the enemy will take turns attacking. Every move costs energy. When the timer rans out, it is considered as pass. The green bar is your health bar, while the while bar is your energy bar.

Controls:

ATTACK: deals small amount of damage

SKill: deals massive amount of damage

Drink: heals a little bit and deals a small amount of damage

Energy Cost:

Attack: 20%
Skill: 100%
Drink: 50%
Pass: 0%


Note: You gain 20% Energy each turn.
When timer reaches to 1, pass will be declared.
